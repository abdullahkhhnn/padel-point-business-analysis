📊 Padel Business Intelligence Dashboard
Overview

The Padel Business Intelligence Dashboard is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project developed using Microsoft Power BI. The objective of this project is to transform raw booking and expense data into meaningful insights that support business decision-making.

The dashboard enables business owners and managers to monitor financial performance, analyze operational efficiency, evaluate customer payment behavior, and identify opportunities to improve profitability.

🎯 Business Problem

Managing a padel business involves tracking multiple operational and financial metrics, including revenue, expenses, court utilization, booking trends, and customer payment preferences.

Without a centralized reporting solution, it becomes difficult to:

Monitor business performance.
Identify high-performing courts.
Understand customer booking behavior.
Track operational efficiency.
Make data-driven business decisions.

This dashboard was designed to solve these challenges by providing an interactive and comprehensive reporting solution.

🎯 Project Objectives
Monitor overall business performance.
Analyze revenue and profitability.

📈 Key Business Insights
Court 1 generated the highest share of total revenue.
Evening time slots experienced the highest court utilization.
Cash remained the most frequently used payment method.
Salaries represented the largest operating expense.
Court-level profitability was estimated using booking-hour-based expense allocation.

💡 Business Recommendations
Revenue
Increase court availability during high-demand evening hours.
Introduce promotional offers during low-demand periods to improve utilization.
Operations
Schedule maintenance during off-peak hours.
Improve utilization of Court 2 through targeted promotions.
Marketing
Encourage digital payments through promotional incentives.
Introduce loyalty or membership programs to increase customer retention.
